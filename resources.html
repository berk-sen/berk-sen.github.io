Marking System

31-52

Regions below 53 points are vulnerable to serious damage in case of floods. These regions should take immediate actions for every field and improve their policies.

53-71

Regions between 71 and 53 points are at a good level in risk reduction.Improvements can be made according to situation in the region.

72-93

Regions above 71 points have a very good general risk reduction and some special measures for critical things.All the regions under high risk flooding events should be on this category.
