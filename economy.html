Economic Impacts of Floods

1-Direct Impacts:

      a)Damages done to the property and land.

      b)Destroyed livelihoods

      c)Disruption of banking systems

      d)Hundreds of lost work hours

      e)Decrease in GDP do the decline of purchasing power


1-Indirect Impacts:

      a)Huge cost of reconstruction efforts

      b)Disruption of outside communities due to migrations

      c)Heavier taxes for efforts like preventing and minimizing flood impacts

      d)Drop in worker productivity due to traumatic experiences

      e)In effective responses cause social disruption


Ways to Minimize Impact

1-In high risk locations governments should offer disaster insurance plans within logical limits.

2-Governments should have fonts for possible reconstruction events

3-Governments should plan different reconstruction strategies according to possible events for maximizing efficiency

4-All nations should gather under an international roof and help each other

5-More money should be spent on preventative thinks like predetermination and minimization of floods
